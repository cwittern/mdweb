    Important note [2015-11-22]: This website is currently under development. Please do not base an evaluation of its content or functionality on the current state, which might change every day. Blackouts and Failures are also expected. Documentation is missing. That said, any feedback that might lead to the improvement of its site and its content is very much appreciated. Anybody who is interested in developing tools that interoperate with this site should contact me through @kanripo on github. A public release is planned for March 2016.

    This website provides access to the texts in the Kanseki Repository. At the moment, there is only very simple search functionality, but it is planned to extend this depending on the needs of the users.

    More information about the Kanseki Repository, its design considerations and aims can be found here: Kanripo Blog.

    A more sophisticated and powerful way to access the repository is through Mandoku, an extension of Org-Mode for Emacs.
